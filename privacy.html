Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2025

🔒
Short version: Fractions Calculator does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Your calculations stay on your device (and in your private iCloud account if you enable sync). We never see them.

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. The application does not collect or send any user data.

Data We Collect

We only collect crash reports sent to us by Apple, and this is data would only be used to fix a bug and issue a software update.

Data typePurposePersonally identifiable?
Crash reports Diagnose and fix bugs No — stack traces only, no user data,
Anonymous usage events Understand which features are used No — aggregated counts, no identifiers
App Store purchase receipt Verify in-app purchases Handled by Apple; we receive only a boolean "purchased/not purchased"

Crash reports and usage events are processed by Apple's built-in frameworks. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs or advertising networks.

Data We Don't Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your calculations, expressions, or results
  • Your calculation history
  • Device location
  • Camera, microphone, or photo library access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any biometric data

iCloud Storage

If you enable iCloud Sync in Settings, your calculation history is stored in your private iCloud account using CloudKit. This means:

  • The data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple.
  • Only you — via devices signed in to your Apple ID — can access it.
  • We have no access to your iCloud data whatsoever.
  • You can disable iCloud Sync at any time in Settings → iCloud Sync.
  • Deleting your iCloud data is done from Settings (iOS) → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Fractions Calculator → Delete Data.

iCloud Sync is opt-in and off by default. If you never enable it, your history remains only on the device where calculations were made and is never uploaded anywhere.

Purchases & Subscriptions

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details, Apple ID, or any billing information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs data related to purchases.

Analytics

We use Apple's built-in analytics tools only. These tools share anonymised, aggregated data with us provided you have opted in to sharing analytics with app developers on your device (Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements). If you have opted out of developer analytics sharing, no analytics data is sent from your device.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar).

Children's Privacy

Fractions Calculator is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations. No parental consent is required for a child to use the app.

Your Rights

Because we do not hold your personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. All user-generated data (calculation history) lives only on your device and in your private iCloud account, both of which you control entirely.

If you have a question about data held by Apple (App Store account, iCloud, purchases), please contact Apple directly at privacy.apple.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, if the changes are material, notify you via an in-app banner on the next launch. Continued use of the app after the updated policy is posted constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@fractionscalculator.app

We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
