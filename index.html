Risk Board Game Calculator

Support pages for the Risk Board Game Calculator App on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Give yourself the tactical advantage in the board game of Risk. Calculate the probability of winning before the battle, or run dice count and army count simulations to establish when to attack your opponent’s border.

The design of the calculator is designed to be as simple and as quick as possible to use. Simply dial in the attacking and defending army count, and the number of dice in play, and the calculator will immediately show you the probabilistic outcome of the battle for both the attacking and defending sides.
